About the CATA Community & TSA Chapter

The CATA Community Resource Hub is a student-led project created by the Central Academy of Technology & Arts TSA Chapter. Our goal is to connect residents with reliable, local support in one easy-to-use website.

This site is updated as new resources and programs become available. While the information is curated by students, we work closely with local organizations to keep listings accurate and helpful.

If you represent an organization in the CATA area, we invite you to submit your service using the form below so we can share it with our neighbors.

Our Focus
